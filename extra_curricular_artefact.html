Extra-Curricular Artefact

The CARL framework was used to structure this reflection!


Content

I have been volunteering with Australian Red Cross - Telecross Support Service since 18 March 2021, and my mission is to provides a daily reassurance telephone call to the frail, aged, people with a disability or medically at risk to check on their health and well-being. If the call is not answered, we initiate an emergency procedure and arrange for assistance. There are 80 senior adults that I call to check their well-being on every Monday morning.


Action

Generally, I start my work by picking a list of clients and call to check their health being for 3 to 6 minutes each. At first, I felt shy to speak because of my accent as an international student, and I talked like a robot; this made my conversation a little bit weird. As time pass by, I overcame my fear and became socially confident and outgoing. I think I could have done it better at the beginning if I gave a vote of confidence in myself.


Result

I could tell that Telecross Support Service is a meaningful job because, as a result of my work, sometimes I can help the elderly people who are ill or has fallen and yet no one notice until my call. And I am glad that I could help. This situation happened in the past, and it will happen again in the future. Thus, it is important for me to ensure that I do not miss any call to my clients. I think this service could be extended larger to support and empower elderly people and communities in times of vulnerability if more youngsters undertake this volunteer.


Learning

Though I feel confident to speak with my clients now, I realise that there are rooms for me to improve in speaking and listening skills because there are times that my clients use slangs or words which I do not know what it means at all. Luckily, my clients are good people, and they patiently teach me those new words with examples so that I could understand them when they use those words again next time. I also spend 30 minutes every morning listening to BBC Learning English program to upgrade my English to ensure that I can offer a smooth service to my present and future clients.
